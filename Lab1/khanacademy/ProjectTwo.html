Pasta

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 200 g
Cream 150 ml
Garlic 2 cloves
Cheese 50 g

Step 1: Boil the pasta in salted water.

Step 2: Fry garlic lightly, add cream.

Step 3: Mix pasta with sauce and add cheese.

Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 2 tbsp

Step 1: Mix all ingredients.

Step 2: Heat the pan.

Step 3: Fry pancakes until golden.
